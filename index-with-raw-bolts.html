About Shivom Fasteners

It was in the year 2008 that Shivom Fasteners came into existence with the aim of providing fastening solutions under one roof. For a complete range of fasteners, the company kept continuing to develop further by establishing adequate testing facilities and R&D section to maintain strict quality standards.

At present, M/S Shivom Fasteners is ISO Certificate 9001:2015 as well as an approved source for various OEM. Shivom Fasteners, having won the trust of OEM customers in highly competitive and quality-conscious markets, grows by increasing its turnover every year. Our aim of TQM is set to achieve the level of customers expectation beyond customer satisfaction.
